Section 04

Cancellation Policy

We understand that travel plans change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 24 hours before pickup: Full refund issued to original payment method within 5–10 business days.
  • 12–24 hours before pickup: 50% refund issued.
  • Less than 12 hours before pickup: No refund. The full charge applies.
  • No-show: Full charge applies. Defined as client not present within 45 minutes of scheduled pickup.
  • Airport transfers: We monitor flight status. Delayed flights are accommodated at no additional charge.
Force Majeure: In the event of cancellation due to acts of nature, government-imposed travel restrictions, or other circumstances beyond our control, the Company will offer a full credit or reschedule at no penalty.
